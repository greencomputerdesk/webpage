Spencer is a writer from Saskatoon living in Vancouver. He has an MFA from the UBC School of Creative Writing. Recently, he was made Geist's nominee for the upcoming Journey Prize for his story "Bloom of Youth". He also plays in the band PC Dagg and has a newsletter.
